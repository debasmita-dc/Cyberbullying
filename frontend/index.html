What is Cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying is the use of digital platforms to harass, threaten, or humiliate individuals repeatedly. It can occur on social media, messaging apps, email, gaming platforms, and more.

Hate Speech

Offensive language targeting someone's identity, appearance, or beliefs.

Stalking

Repeated unwanted contact, monitoring, or following online.

Threats

Direct or implied threats of physical harm or violence.

Privacy Violation

Sharing private images, personal info, or recordings without consent.
