Legal Support & Awareness

Key Indian laws that protect victims of cyberbullying and online harassment. Know your rights.

  • IPC 503 — Criminal intimidation — threats of injury to person, reputation, or property.
  • IPC 509 — Insult to modesty — words, gestures, or acts intended to insult dignity.
  • IPC 354D — Stalking / cyber stalking — repeated following, contacting, or monitoring.
  • IPC 354A — Sexual harassment — unwelcome sexually-tinted behavior or demands.
  • IT Act 66E — Violation of privacy — capturing or publishing private images online.
  • IT Act 67A — Sexually explicit content — publishing or transmitting obscene material electronically.
  • IT Act 67B — Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) — strictest penalties; report immediately.
  • Cyber Crime Portal — Online FIR & reporting at cybercrime.gov.in
